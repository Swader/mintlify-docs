To brige Eth into Bokuto from Sepolia, use
[this contract](https://sepolia.etherscan.io/address/0x528e26b25a34a4A5d0dbDa1d57D318153d2ED582#writeProxyContract),
`bridgeAsset` function. Payable amount is amount of Eth to bridge without
decimals (i.e. 5), destination network 37, amount is amount with decimals (i.e.
5000000000000000000), token address is
`0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000` for Eth, force update is `true` and
`permitData` is `0x`. Once submitted, the transfer will be processsed in 10 - 30
minutes.
